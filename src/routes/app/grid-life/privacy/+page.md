**Grid Life · Make It Count**

Last updated: March 2, 2026

## The short version

Grid Life does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

## Data storage

All data you create in Grid Life — your day entries, milestones, notes, effort ratings, and visual preferences — is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults and App Groups).

If you use the home screen widget, your settings are shared with the widget through a local App Group container (`group.ee.lsdigital.app.gridlife`). This data never leaves your device.

## iCloud sync

Grid Life offers optional iCloud sync using Apple's key-value storage (`NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore`). When enabled, your settings and day entries are synced across your devices through your personal iCloud account.

- iCloud sync is **on by default** but can be disabled at any time in the app settings
- Synced data is handled entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure
- We have no access to your iCloud data
- Apple's own privacy policy governs how iCloud data is handled: [apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

## Data we collect

We may collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g. which features are used most and how often the app is opened) to help improve Grid Life. This data is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to identify you.

We do not collect:

- Advertising identifiers
- Device identifiers
- Personal information (name, email, location)

## Third-party services

Grid Life does not integrate with any ad networks or external APIs. We may use privacy-focused third-party services for analytics and purchase management. Any such services are used solely to improve the app and process transactions — never to sell or share your data.

## Purchases

Grid Life may offer optional paid features. All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information — Apple handles all transaction details. Purchase-related information may be managed by a third-party service solely to verify and restore your purchases.

## Children's privacy

Grid Life does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.
