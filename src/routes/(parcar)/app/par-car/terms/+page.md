## 2. The Service

Par Car is a premium car-washing service offered to members of participating golf clubs. Members book washes via the Par Car iOS app. Our crew receives the vehicle on the club's premises during the member's round, performs the chosen service, and returns the vehicle to its parking spot before the member's expected return.

## 3. Eligibility

## 5. Service tiers & pricing

We offer three service tiers:

- **Express Exterior** — €45 · ~30 min · hand wash, wheels, tire shine
- **Full Valet** — €85 · ~60 min · interior + exterior detail
- **Signature Detail** — €140 · ~90 min · clay bar, leather conditioning, paint sealant

Prices include VAT. We may adjust prices with 30 days notice via the app.

## 6. Payment

Payment is taken at the time of booking via Stripe. We do not see or store your card details. Tips are optional and passed in full to the crew. All transactions are in EUR.

## 7. Cancellation policy

- **Free cancellation** if you cancel at least 60 minutes before the wash is scheduled to begin
- **50% refund** if you cancel between 60 and 30 minutes before
- **No refund** if you cancel less than 30 minutes before, or once the wash has begun

Refunds are issued via the original payment method within 5–10 business days.
