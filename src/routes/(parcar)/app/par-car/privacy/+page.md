**Par Car · Premium car care, while you play**

Last updated: 25 June 2026

## The short version

Par Car collects only the data we need to wash your car. Your name, the vehicle, the booking, payment processed by Stripe, and a finished-car photo. We do not sell your data. **Clubs never see individual member records** — they receive aggregate reporting only (volume, adoption, satisfaction).

## Data we collect

**Account data**
- Name (first, last)
- Email (optional, used for receipts and account recovery)
- Phone number (optional, used for status notifications)
- Club membership info (your club, member ID if you provide it)
- Apple ID identifier when you use Sign in with Apple

**Vehicle data**
- Make, model, year, color, and licence plate of each vehicle you register

**Booking data**
- Service tier chosen, scheduled time, parking spot
- Key-drop preference (bag staff, pro shop, or in car)
- Current status throughout the lifecycle (booked → received → washing → drying → ready → picked up)
- Crew assigned to your wash
- Tip and rating you provide

**Service-record data**
- A photograph of your vehicle taken after the wash, attached to your booking as proof of service

**Payment data**
- Card details are handled exclusively by **Stripe** (PCI DSS Level 1 certified). We never see, store, or transmit raw card numbers. We retain only a transaction reference for accounting.

**Device & technical data**
- Anonymous usage analytics (which features are used, app launches) — not linked to your identity
- Crash reports from Apple's standard crash diagnostics
- Apple Push Notification token, only when you opt in to status updates

**What we do NOT collect**
- GPS location (the parking spot is text you enter; we do not track your phone)
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- Contacts, photo library, or any on-device data outside the app
- Health, financial, or biometric data

## How we use this data

- Deliver your booking — the crew needs your name, vehicle, parking spot, and key-drop preference
- Charge your payment method via Stripe and issue receipts
- Send status notifications via Apple Push Notification service when you opt in
- Provide service-record photos in case of dispute about the wash
- Generate aggregate reporting for partner clubs (totals, averages, satisfaction — no individuals)
- Improve the app through anonymous usage analytics

## Who has access to your data

- **You** — everything is visible inside your Par Car account
- **Par Car's crew** — only what's needed to perform your wash (member name, vehicle make/model/colour, parking spot, key-drop method)
- **The Club** — receives **aggregate reporting only** (total bookings, total spend, average ticket, NPS). The Club does not see your name, your vehicle, your bookings, or your photos.
- **Stripe** — processes the payment, holds card details on their PCI-compliant infrastructure
- **Apple** — handles Sign in with Apple, push notifications, and App Store transactions
- **Nobody else.** We do not sell or rent personal data. We do not use it for advertising.

## Third-party services

- [Apple Inc.](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) — Sign in with Apple, push notifications, App Store
- [Stripe, Inc.](https://stripe.com/privacy) — payment processing, PCI DSS Level 1
- (Future) A privacy-focused analytics provider for anonymous usage metrics

## Your rights under GDPR

As a Par Car user in the EU, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you
- **Rectification** — correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Erasure** — delete your account and associated data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability** — export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object** — opt out of specific processing
- **Restrict** — limit how we use your data
- **Withdraw consent** — for any processing based on consent
- **Lodge a complaint** with the Estonian Data Protection Inspectorate (Andmekaitse Inspektsioon)

To exercise any of these rights, email [info@lsdigital.ee](mailto:info@lsdigital.ee). We respond within 30 days.

## Data retention

- **Account data** — kept while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account closure.
- **Booking history & payment records** — retained for **7 years** to comply with Estonian tax law.
- **Service-record photos** — 90 days after the booking, then permanently deleted.
- **Anonymous analytics** — 24 months.

## Security

- All data in transit uses HTTPS / TLS 1.3 or higher
- Card data is tokenised by Stripe — never stored on our systems
- Account access requires Sign in with Apple
- Internal access to member data is limited to the minimum necessary for service delivery and is logged

## Children's privacy

Par Car is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used Par Car, contact us and we will delete their data.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy materially changes, we will notify active users by email and via an in-app notice, and update the "last updated" date at the top.
